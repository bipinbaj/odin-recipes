Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey cheese. It's a comforting and hearty dish that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.
  2. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer, uncovered, for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Spoon a third of the meat sauce into a greased 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer with half of the noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  5. Cover and bake at 375°F for 45 minutes. Uncover; bake 10 minutes longer or until bubbly. Let stand for 15 minutes before cutting.
